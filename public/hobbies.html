Hobbies and Passion

Corrida

Running in the late afternoon in Rio de Janeiro is one of my favorite activities. The city's stunning landscapes, with its iconic beaches and mountains, provide the perfect backdrop for an invigorating run. As the sun sets, painting the sky with hues of orange and pink, I find a sense of peace and fulfillment. The cool breeze and the sound of the waves create a refreshing atmosphere, making each run a truly enjoyable and revitalizing experience.

Futebol

I have a deep passion for playing soccer. There's something incredibly exhilarating about the sport that draws me in every time. The thrill of the game, the strategy involved, and the camaraderie with teammates make every match special. Whether it's a casual game with friends or a more competitive setting, soccer always brings me joy and excitement.
